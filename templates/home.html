How It Works

Post a Task

Quickly post your urgent needs, from picking up a delivery to borrowing a book. Set a tip for the helper to show your appreciation.

Get Help Instantly

Peers in your campus community will be notified of your task. The first one to accept it gets the reward.

Build Trust

Helpers earn a reputation and ratings for every completed task, ensuring accountability and building a reliable community.
